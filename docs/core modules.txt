the four core modules of AI-Based Smart Lawyer are:

AI Chatbot with Accurate Citations:
Answers user queries using RAG, with verifiable citations linked to the original legal sources.

Judgment Summarization:
Automatically summarizes lengthy court judgments into concise, plain-language explanations.

Contract Summarization:
Allows users to upload contracts; the system extracts, analyzes, and summarizes key clauses and risks.

Legal Search (Laws & Judgments):
Enables users to search civil laws and judgments directly, with links to the authentic sources (Pakistan Code, court portals, etc.).
